Family and Home

To me, your family is defined by those who, regardless of what circumstances life may bring, will always be there when you need them. Irrespective of how it comes to be, you share a bond that no amount of time, arguments, distance, or any other factors can break.

I've moved a lot throughout my life, so I never really developed a place I would call "home." On any given day, I would be ready to pack up my stuff and move across the world without a second thought. Because of this, however, I've learned to always appreciate my time with the people around me regardless of where I am. Although I may have no idea what life has in store for me, I know I'll at the very least make the best of it.

Entertainment

I think to be a more complete human you have to learn from as many different people and cultures as you can. I’ve always been fascinated by how one’s culture affects them due to growing up split between cultures. This fascination defines how I use media today. There is no better way to learn about a people and a culture than by consuming the media they produce.

Community

I always feel the most at home with people who are incredibly passionate about what they do, regardless of what that may be. Seeing and being around that energy keeps me from being complacent and pushes me to follow my passions as well.

School

I’ve always loved learning but growing up I despised school. This disconnect meant that if there was something I was interested in I had to learn it by myself. Although it was something that caused me a lot of frustration when I was younger, understanding how to learn on my own has served me very well in the years since.
